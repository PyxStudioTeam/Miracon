Golden Visa

EU residency through property investment — from €250,000

Greek Residency

Greece offers one of Europe's most accessible residency-by-investment programmes. Purchase qualifying real estate and receive a 5-year renewable residence permit for your entire family — no minimum stay required

Investment threshold

The minimum qualifying investment is €250,000. Both Esopou 19 and Monastiriou 4 in central Thessaloniki meet this threshold — making MIRACON projects an ideal entry point into the programme

Family included

The residence permit covers the investor, spouse, children under 21, and both sets of parents. No additional fees per family member. The permit is renewed every 5 years as long as the property is owned

Schengen access

As a Greek residence permit holder you can travel freely across 26 Schengen countries without a visa. No minimum stay in Greece required — visit when you wish, live where you want
